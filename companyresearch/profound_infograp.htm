Growth & Sales Strategy

Profound's growth is likely driven by a hybrid strategy combining product-led growth (PLG) with targeted content marketing and a direct sales force for enterprise clients. This approach captures a wide segment of the market, from individual users discovering the product on their own to large teams requiring a comprehensive solution.

  • ●Content Marketing: Attracts users by providing value through expert articles, webinars, and guides on user research best practices.
  • ●Product-Led Growth (PLG): Utilizes a free trial or freemium model to let the product's value drive adoption and conversion from the bottom up.
  • ●Direct Sales: A dedicated team engages with large, high-value accounts to address complex needs and close enterprise deals.
  • ●SEO & Organic Search: Targets relevant keywords to capture high-intent traffic from researchers and product managers looking for solutions.
